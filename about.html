my journey

graduated with a Bachelor's of Business Administration in 2014, i was fortunate to set foot in the Technology industry. during the last 4 years, i have been rotating in different positions: from marketing to business development, from product design to business analytics. these experiences have opened up my eyes with various business models and different perspectives of stakeholders, also helped me to understand the scope of work and requirements for different majors.

to the best of my recollection, my interest in data initiated since my job as a digital marketing specialist. in the last two years working as a business analyst at ChoTot, the largest online classified website in Vietnam, this interest has truly elaborated and transformed into a passion. i was completely amazed by how every single decision had to be made upon data analysis, and how the system was carefully built and optimized to generate the most accurate and reliable data. it was such a wonderful experience, but never a smooth journey. i remembered feeling ecstatic and proud of my reporting solutions being used company-wide. however, i came to an understanding that having a background in business and user insights was an advantage, but the requirement to develop my competencies was more than what meets the eye.

being aware of the requirements for further career development, i decided to move to Canada to continue to learn and develop as a professional in Data Analytics. i am currently taking full-time training in the Big Data Analytics post-graduate program at Georgian College in Barrie, Ontario and will be graduating in April 2019.

looking back at the past few years, i am truly grateful for all the amazing people and exciting experiences that brought me here, helped me to discover my passion and enabled me to obtain a clear vision of my career path. i have the certainty that the adventure will continue to be thrilling, rewarding and i look forward to having your company as the next chapter of this fruitful journey.
